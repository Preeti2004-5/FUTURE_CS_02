Phishing Awareness & Prevention Guide

What is Phishing?
Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers send fake emails or messages to trick people into giving passwords, OTPs, or money.

Common Phishing Signs:
- Urgent messages like “Act now” or “Account will be blocked”
- Unknown or fake email domains
- Poor grammar or spelling
- Links that look suspicious
- Requests for OTP, password, or bank details

How to Stay Safe:

1. Check Sender Carefully
- Look at full email address, not just name
- Be careful of fake domains

2. Do Not Click Unknown Links
- Hover over links to see real address
- Avoid clicking links in urgent emails

3. Never Share OTP or Password
- No company asks for OTP or password by email

4. Verify Before Acting
- Call or visit official website directly

5. Use Spam Filters
- Do not open emails from spam folder

6. Report Phishing
- Mark email as spam or phishing

Conclusion:
Being careful and checking emails properly can prevent most phishing attacks.
