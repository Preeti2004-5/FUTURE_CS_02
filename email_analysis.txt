Email Sample 1: Job Listings 4U

Subject:
Looking for a SOC analyst job?

Sender:
info@joblistings4u.com

Folder:
Spam

Observations:
- Found in spam folder
- Generic subject line
- Unknown sender domain
- Looks like mass job advertisement

Suspicious Signs:
- Not from official company domain
- No personalization
- Promises jobs easily
- Looks like bulk mail

Conclusion:
This email is likely phishing or spam.
Risk Level: High

Subject:

samsung@in.email.samsung.com

Inbox

Legitimate Signs:
- Official-looking domain
- Branding and formatting is professional
- No demand for personal details
- No urgent “act now or lose account” message
